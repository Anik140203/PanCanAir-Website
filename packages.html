COMMON
QUESTIONS.

How does per-unit pricing work?
Your monthly cost = your unit count × the program rate (Essential $22/unit, Preferred $34/unit). A 60-unit building on Preferred = $2,040/mo (~$24,480/yr) — typically less than one avoided emergency replacement. Final pricing is confirmed after a free building audit where we verify unit count, assess equipment condition, and scope visit frequency. No hidden adjustments after signing.
What's included in a "preventive maintenance visit"?
Each visit covers a full inspection of your HVAC systems — cleaning coils, checking refrigerant levels, inspecting belts and bearings, testing controls, and replacing filters. You'll receive a written service report after every visit that's detailed enough for your condo board or property management company.
How does emergency dispatch work?
Preferred and Enterprise clients have a direct line to our team around the clock. When you call, a real person picks up and dispatches accordingly. Response timing depends on the nature of the issue — we prioritize based on life-safety risk and building impact, not just ticket order.
Can I switch programs as my building changes?
Yes. Programs are reviewed annually and can be adjusted at renewal. If your building grows — through additional units, major equipment installs, or if you take on a new property — we'll rescope and reprice accordingly with no penalty for upgrading.
Do you work with property management companies managing multiple buildings?
Yes — Enterprise is designed specifically for this. We have PMs managing 3–15 buildings under a single consolidated contract, with one invoice, volume pricing, and coordinated scheduling across properties. Reach out for a portfolio scope.
What geographic area do you serve?
PanCanAir primarily serves the Greater Toronto Area — Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Oakville, Burlington, North York, Scarborough, and surrounding municipalities. Contact us for service areas beyond these boundaries.
